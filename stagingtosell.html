STAGING to SELL
Our Home Staging® expertise has helped many homeowners in the Charlotte area sell their properties quickly, and for the best possible price.

Home Staging® goes beyond how you live in a house; instead, it’s a specialized approach to market your property. 

It gives homeowners a competitive edge by showing off a home’s potential, enhancing its most sellable features, and ultimately appealing to more buyers in the market.

Top 5 Ways We Can Help

Sell Faster – When staged by Domaine Staging, your home will likely sell in half the usual time. In fact, 75% of our clients’ homes sell within 10-15 days following our staging.

Sell for More – Nationally, homes sold through Home Staging® sell for up to 7% more than comparable properties.

Increase Value – With Home Staging®, appraisers are more likely to appraise your home at maximum value. In addition, building inspectors typically view these homes as cared-for properties.

Attract More Buyers – Domaine Staging improves listing options because we list on the StagedHomes® website, which attracts more than 11 million visits each month.

Attract More Agents – We market your home more effectively by introducing your property to our large network of agents.

All of these benefits – and more – are included in the cost of your Home Staging®. There are no extra or hidden costs.
