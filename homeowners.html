HOMEOWNERS
When you are ready to sell your home, we need to start thinking of your house as a product.  After all, when a product is marketed in a visually pleasing way, at the right price, it attracts more buyers and results in a faster sale. That’s the concept behind Home Staging®.

A Staged home sells faster
On average, a property supported by Home Staging® sells in just 35 days, compared to 177 days for the non-staged home. We realized that it’s difficult for homeowners to make major changes when selling their property. Memories, personal belongings, favorite furniture – they all make it hard to be objective. That’s where an Accredited Staging Professional can help.

The WOW factor
We can arrange furniture to create better flow or address space challenges.  We look for visual pop to add drama. And we create a WOW factor to make your home memorable to the buyer. More importantly, we know how to appeal to a greater number of buyers by having them see themselves living in that property.

Work with you
Domaine Staging works with you to make the tough decisions.  Personal belongings may need to be stored, and various spaces may need to be de-cluttered so that buyers see their potential.

One-stop shop
As a full-service shop, we have a wide range of inventory and can coordinate rental furniture, accessories, and help with a pre-move, if necessary. Our many affiliates help us handle logistics and schedule contractors to paint, re-carpet, repair, clean or landscape.

Unsurpassed payoff
While the process may seem excessive or difficult, the payoff is unsurpassed. We believe that a staged home will sell faster at the best price possible so the modifications pay for themselves. And you can get into your next dream home sooner!
