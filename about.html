ABOUT DOMAINE STAGING™
Domaine Staging was established in 2006, before most Charlotte homeowners were introduced to the distinct advantages of Home Staging®. Since then, we have become the Charlotte area leader in this specialized service, having staged more than $60 million worth of property.

 

Earning Trust
We have earned the trust of real estate agents, investors, builders and homeowners alike as we continue to build our business through referrals and word-of-mouth. We often are featured as an expert source on real estate trends in news articles and TV segments throughout the region.

 

Marcyne Touchton
As the Founder and Principal Stager for Domaine Staging, Marcyne Touchton is one of only 140 Accredited Staging Professional Masters in North America. This exclusive designation is officially recognized by the National Association of Realtors.

 

Marcyne is known as an avid proponent of aggressive marketing, which is evident in her success – 75% of her stagings result in home sales in 15 days or less.

 

Trained by Barb Schwarz
Marcyne was trained personally by Barb Schwarz, who pioneered Home Staging® and is the founder of Stagedhomes.com.

 

Following her training, Marcyne founded Domaine Staging and promptly introduced the Home Staging® concept to the Charlotte market, where it has been eagerly embraced.

 

Featured Expert
As a home staging expert, Marcyne is a featured speaker before many business and industry organizations, real estate offices, and community groups. If you are interested in having Marcyne come to do a presentation, please contact her.  

 

Marcyne is married to Brian Touchton, who is her business manager, and has three children.  When not staging, she admits to dreaming about scuba diving off the Fiji Islands.
