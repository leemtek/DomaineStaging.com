CONSULTATION - DIY STAGING

The home selling process can be quite exciting yet overwhelming. Domaine can help with the process by giving you a consultation report, which is a comprehensive written report of what you need to do to do the Staging yourself. If you are a hands-on type of person with time to implement the staging, or are just looking for saving some costs, the constulation report is for you.

Consultations begin with a two to four hour thorough walk-through of the exterior and interior of the home. We will take digital photos to highlight areas of concern and/or properly arrange your furniture and accessories. We will review the steps of Staging and discuss how each step will add to the property's overall allure. You will learn about the psychology of the buyer and how your home can best appeal to the buyer’s five senses. You will also discover why Staged homes have a competitive advantage over homes not prepared for sale, and why each recommendation is important to your overall goal of selling your home for top dollar.

We will then begin preparing your written Home Evaluation, which is a comprehensive 20plus-page interior and exterior diagnostic report with specific enhancement instructions.

The Home Evaluation includes:

1. Customized, room-by-room enhancement recommendations that you can implement yourself

2. Multiple property photos with detailed information on how to improve the space

3. Recommendations of any repairs or improvements that will benefit the property

4. Suggestions on how to prepare the home to sell

5. A list of your Top Five Staging Priorities

Armed with the Home Evaluation, you can immediately begin doing the work yourself or hiring the painters, cleaners, packers and/or handymen you need to get it done and get your house on the market!

We offer a Verbal Consultation which is a complete walk-through of the interior and exterior of the home. We will discuss strengths and weaknesses of the property, as well as suggestions and recommendations you will need to prepare your home for sale. There will be no written report, just the notes you take during the discussion. Verbal Consultations as low as $175!
